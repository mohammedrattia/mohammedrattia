<h1 align="center">Hi, I'm Mohammed R. Attia</h1>
<h3 align="center">A machine learning engineer and data scientist from Egypt</h3>

I am Mohammed Ragab Saad Attia, an alumni of the STEM High School for Boys in Giza, Egypt, with a passion for computer science, data analysis, and machine learning. I am also an active competitive programmer from 2020. I won the 2023 National ISEF in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics. As president of the STEM Egypt Hack Club, I mentored students in competitive programming and machine learning. I am committed to innovation and education, consistently demonstrating leadership and excellence in my field.
